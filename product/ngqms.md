## 💥 Why Queue Management Matters

Customers don’t just wait — they **experience the wait**.

In many businesses, especially customer-facing operations, unmanaged queues create frustration, inefficiency, and negative perception.

Global studies consistently highlight the impact of structured queue management:

- 📉 Up to **30–40% reduction in perceived waiting time**
- 📈 Around **20–30% improvement in staff productivity**
- 😊 Over **70% of customers prefer structured digital queue systems**

**References:**
- McKinsey & Company – Customer experience research  
- PwC – Future of customer experience  
- Nielsen – Consumer behavior insights  

> A well-managed queue is not just operational — it is part of your customer experience.

---

## 🧠 What is NGQMS Lite?

NGQMS Lite is a **cloud-based queue management solution** designed to bring structure, clarity, and efficiency into daily operations.

It replaces manual queue handling with a simple, digital workflow that improves both customer experience and staff productivity.

NGQMS is designed to be:
- Simple to deploy  
- Easy to operate  
- Flexible to scale  

> No server. No complexity. Just results.
